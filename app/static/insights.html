How to Use Predictions

✓ Confidence Intervals

Every prediction includes a confidence range (predicted ± MAE). For example, a prediction of £1,000 with MAE of £170 means the true value is likely between £830-£1,170. Use the full range for planning rather than treating predictions as exact.

✓ Segment-Based Actions

VIP (£10K+): Assign dedicated account managers, offer exclusive perks
High Value (£2K-£10K): Early product access, premium support
Growth (£500-£2K): Upsell campaigns, loyalty programs
At Risk (<£500): Re-engagement emails, win-back offers

✓ Risk Assessment

Combine predictions with recency to identify churn risk. High recency (180+ days) + low predicted revenue = critical churn risk. Act immediately with targeted campaigns.

✓ Batch Processing

For operational workflows, use the batch API endpoint or CSV upload to predict revenue for your entire customer base. Integrate with your CRM or BI tools for automated reporting.
